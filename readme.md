# Patra | Where Hearts Align
In a world of fleeting glances and instantaneous judgments, Patra emerges as a sanctuary for genuine connection. We are not just another dating app; we are a movement dedicated to looking beyond the superficial. Patra, derived from a word meaning 'vessel' or 'worthy recipient', is built on the philosophy that a person's true essence—their thoughts, passions, and kindness—is infinitely more beautiful than a curated profile picture.

Our mission is to create a space where your heart is your best feature and your personality is your profile picture. We believe that meaningful relationships are forged in the fires of shared values, late-night conversations, and spontaneous adventures, not just mutual attraction.

## 💖 Our Guiding Philosophy: The Heart's Glimpse

We've fundamentally redesigned the matching experience to prioritize what truly matters.

**We don't care about how people look, but how their heart is.**

How do we do this? With our unique **"Glimpse"** system. When you first encounter a profile, the photos are artistically blurred, like a memory you can't quite place. Instead, you are greeted with their answers to introspective prompts, their recent thoughts, and the causes they care about. As you interact—by appreciating their thoughts or engaging in conversation—the Glimpse slowly fades, revealing the person behind the personality. It’s a journey of discovery, ensuring that by the time you see someone clearly, you've already begun to know their soul.

## ✨ Unique Features That Set Us Apart

Patra combines the best elements of modern social apps and re-imagines them for deeper, more authentic human connection.

### 📡 Signals: Seize the Moment
Tired of endless swiping for a "maybe someday" connection? **Signals** are ephemeral, real-time status updates for what you're thinking, feeling, or doing *right now*.
-   Post a Signal like: *"Heading to the botanical gardens for a walk. Anyone want to join and talk about the meaning of life or the best local coffee?"*
-   Or something simpler: *"In the mood for some live music tonight in the city center. Any recommendations or company?"*
-   These Signals are broadcasted to people nearby for a limited time (e.g., 6 hours). Others can react and reply directly, creating a low-pressure opportunity for a spontaneous, temporary connection. It's about sharing a moment, not committing to a lifetime.

### 🧭 The Soul Compass: Proximity with Purpose
Of course, you want to meet people nearby. But instead of just showing you who's within a 5km radius, our **Soul Compass** uses a smart algorithm that suggests people based on a "Vibe Affinity Score."
-   It considers your location, your personality traits (derived from your prompts), your active interests, and even the time of day you're most active.
-   The Compass guides you towards serendipitous encounters—the person reading your favorite author in a cafe just a block away, or someone whose 'Signal' for adventure matches your own.

### 📝 The Unfolding Profile: More Than Just a Bio
Static profiles are boring. A person is a story that's constantly being written.
-   **Introspective Prompts:** We replace the standard bio with a series of thought-provoking questions that change weekly. "What song currently lives in your head rent-free?", "What's a small act of kindness you witnessed recently?", "Describe a perfect, quiet morning."
-   **Heartbeats:** A feed on your profile showcasing things you've recently loved—a song from Spotify, a book you've finished, an article you found insightful. This creates a living, breathing profile that reflects who you are today.

### 🌟 Constellations: Connect Through Passions
Find your tribe before you find your person. **Constellations** are user-created micro-communities based on shared interests.
-   Join the `Stargazers of Patiala`, `Indie Cinema Lovers`, or `Weekend Hikers` Constellations.
-   Within these groups, you can have discussions, plan group events, and connect with people in a platonic-first environment. A beautiful friendship might just be the best foundation for something more.

## 💻 Tech Stack & Architecture

Patra is built with a modern, scalable, and delightful technology stack to ensure a smooth and responsive user experience.

-   **Frontend:** `React.js` & `React Native` (for eventual mobile deployment)
-   **Language:** `TypeScript` for robust, type-safe code.
-   **Styling:** `Tailwind CSS` for rapid, utility-first UI development.
-   **State Management:** `Redux Toolkit` for predictable and efficient state handling.
-   **Backend:** `Node.js` with `Express.js` (or your chosen backend).
-   **Database:** `PostgreSQL` with `Prisma ORM`.
-   **Real-time Communication:** `WebSockets` for Signals and chat features.

## 🚀 Getting Started

Want to get the development environment running on your local machine? Follow these simple steps.

### Prerequisites

-   Node.js (v18.x or higher)
-   npm / yarn / pnpm
-   A `.env` file with the necessary environment variables (see `.env.example`).

### Installation

1.  **Clone the repository:**
    ```bash
    git clone [https://github.com/your-username/patra.git](https://github.com/your-username/patra.git)
    ```

2.  **Navigate to the project directory:**
    ```bash
    cd patra
    ```

3.  **Install dependencies:**
    ```bash
    npm install
    ```

4.  **Run the development server:**
    ```bash
    npm run dev
    ```

5.  Open [http://localhost:3000](http://localhost:3000) with your browser to see the result!
